# Stories — torso-first overhaul

Generated from a design→critique→revise→synthesize workflow (19 agents). Each story was
redesigned so the **physical pump is the instrument**: tap-a-hypothesis → the tank answers,
stillness = information, at least one read-the-body decision, and one tank-surprise twist —
keeping each story's topic and depth. Physical pump only (no on-screen vessel).

## The six (build order)

| Order | Story | id | Signature verb |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Die Blut-Balance | [`ddi`](ddi.md) | SCAN-A-PAIRING -> THE TANK ANSWERS (stillness-vs-surge) |
| 2 | Die Frühstücks-Falle | [`fruehstuecks-falle`](fruehstuecks-falle.md) | WEGNEHMEN-UND-DAS-WASSER-LESEN |
| 3 | Die Codein-Werkstatt — Eine Pille, drei Körper | [`gene`](gene.md) | PREDICT-THEN-POUR auf EINEM Tank, dreimal, mit Körpertausch dazwischen |
| 4 | Der müde Filter | [`organ`](organ.md) | CALIBRATE-A-LIVE-INFLOW-AGAINST-AN-INVISIBLE-WEAKENED-DRAIN |
| 5 | Der Wochen-Pillenplan: Halt den Rhythmus | [`adherence`](adherence.md) | COMPOSE-A-WEEK, THEN TURN THE DAYS BY HAND |
| 6 | Das pflanzliche Leck | [`johanniskraut`](johanniskraut.md) | LECK LESEN & STOPFEN durch ZEIT |

## Why this order

1. **ddi** — Build FIRST as the reference. Its SCAN-A-PAIRING (still·still·still·SURGE) is the purest expression of every grammar rule at once — tap a hypothesis, read stillness vs surge, one mandatory twist demo where 'the safe pill changes nothing on the body.' Nail the shared <WatchBody/> banner, the testHypothesis(target,isReal) stillness helper, the EndScreen handoff, and the canonical red-line copy HERE; every other story reuses them. Bonus: the current DdiPlay is the most anti-grammar file in the repo (PairLink quiz + bin-sort + floating 🩸), so rebuilding it proves the new pattern end-to-end.
2. **fruehstuecks-falle** — Build SECOND — it is the same tap→stillness-or-move loop as ddi but POLARITY-FLIPPED (remove→water falls). Building it right after ddi forces you to harden the shared helper against both directions and to lock the ddi/fruehstuecks copy split (scannen vs wegnehmen) while both are fresh, killing the set's worst collision early.
3. **gene** — Build THIRD. Reuses the stillness loop but adds the predict-then-pour wager and the body-swap reset ritual on ONE tank — a new shared mechanic (bet card before the move). It also retires the on-screen mini-bars, validating the 'no mirrored vessel' lint rule on the worst current offender.
4. **organ** — Build FOURTH. First story needing LIVE rising water + a timing-window catch (tap 'reduzieren' mid-rise) and the auto-trip safety caps (78→79.3). Do it before the two week-stories because it proves the continuous-drive + auto-trip choreography they also lean on.
5. **adherence** — Build FIFTH. Author-the-week + hand-crank one driveTo per tap; depends on the day-by-day playback machinery and the additive 'more drug always pushes UP' loss math. Establishes the multi-day pattern that johanniskraut then differentiates against.
6. **johanniskraut** — Build LAST. Hardest because its signature is a DELAYED, back-dated fall plus a real-time leak defence, and it must be deliberately differentiated from adherence (react-under-pressure vs compose) AND from organ (down-lag vs up-rate). Also the one story whose scoring currently violates the stars() shape — fix that as part of this build once the canonical scoring is settled across the other five.

## Distinctness matrix

Six signature verbs laid side by side on five axes — INPUT GESTURE · WHO MOVES THE PUMP · TANK DIRECTION · TIME MODEL · WHAT STILLNESS MEANS. Each row is unique on at least two axes; no two share an identical (gesture × direction × time) triple.

1) fruehstuecks-falle — REMOVE-AND-READ. Gesture: lift ONE item off the tray. Pump: player-triggered per lift. Direction: DOWN (back into green) or NO MOVE. Time: instant cause→effect. Stillness = "this item is innocent." Unique: the only SUBTRACTIVE verb (you take things AWAY) and the only one where the saving action moves water DOWN/keeps it resting.

2) johanniskraut — READ-A-DELAYED-LEAK-AND-REVERSE. Gesture: place the same tea on an early vs late day, then defend in real time. Pump: time-cranked then live. Direction: DOWN only, but DELAYED (cause Tue, fall Thu→Sun). Time: LAGGED cause→effect; effect GROWS over days. Stillness = nothing (this story has no stillness beat — its signature is the back-dated fall). Unique: the only LAG/back-dating verb; you must blame a day where the water DIDN'T move.

3) gene — PREDICT-THEN-POUR, same pill ×3 with body-swap. Gesture: bet first ("zu wenig/genau/zu viel"), then drop the IDENTICAL pill. Pump: player-triggered, 3 reps on ONE tank with a reset ritual between. Direction: all three (stuck / into green / over the red). Time: instant. Stillness = "this body has no machine for it." Unique: the only REPEAT-THE-SAME-GESTURE-FOR-DIFFERENT-ANSWERS verb; the wager is the mechanic.

4) ddi — SCAN-A-PAIRING (still·still·still·still·SURGE). Gesture: lock ONE fixed probe (new antibiotic), tap it against each existing pill. Pump: player-triggered per scan. Direction: HELD-STILL ×4 then UP on its own. Time: instant. Stillness = "vertragen sich" (the answer IS the non-move, four times in a row). Unique: the only FIXED-PROBE-VS-MANY verb and the only one that weaponises a RUN of stillnesses to make one surge land.

5) organ — CALIBRATE-A-LIVE-INFLOW vs an invisible weakened drain. Gesture: turn ONE inflow tap; tap "reduzieren" at the right MOMENT during a live rise. Pump: player-triggered then continuously rising. Direction: UP toward red, caught and settled into green. Time: continuous/live (rate vs rate). Stillness = N/A (the danger is that it WON'T stop on its own). Unique: the only RATE-AGAINST-RATE verb and the only timing-window catch.

6) adherence — COMPOSE-A-WEEK then HAND-CRANK the days. Gesture: author a 7-slot plan with zero time pressure, then "Nächster Tag" once per day. Pump: player-triggered, one driveTo per tap, carving a saw-tooth. Direction: saw-tooth (down on gaps, up on doses; a panic-double climbs the WRONG way toward red). Time: AUTHORED time-axis (you design the curve before it plays). Stillness = "stop the pill entirely" makes the pump not move ("that's not the danger"). Unique: the only AUTHOR-THE-TIMELINE verb; the curve is the player's own composition.

Bottom line: gene=bet, ddi=scan-pairing, organ=catch-a-rate, adherence=author-time, fruehstuecks=subtract, johanniskraut=back-date-a-lag. Six genuinely different relationships to the water.

## Remaining collisions to watch

- fruehstuecks-falle vs ddi — BOTH are 'tap an item, the pump answers with stillness-or-move, the dangerous one is gated last.' This is the sharpest collision in the set. SEPARATE on POLARITY and GESTURE: fruehstuecks is SUBTRACTIVE (you REMOVE items; the right move makes water FALL/rest) while ddi is a FIXED-PROBE PAIRING (you keep one probe locked and SCAN it against others; the right move makes water RISE on its own). Lock the copy so fruehstuecks never says 'tippe/scanne' and ddi never says 'nimm weg' — fruehstuecks verb is strictly WEGNEHMEN, ddi verb is strictly SCANNEN/PRUEFEN. Also keep fruehstuecks' answer-direction DOWN-or-still and ddi's SURGE-or-still so the tank reads opposite even when the gesture rhythm (innocent·innocent·CULPRIT) rhymes.
- ddi (still·still·still·SURGE) vs gene (stuck body = a stillness) — both use a non-moving tank as a 'not it / no machine' answer. SEPARATE on AGENCY: in ddi the player did NOT predict, the stillness is a discovered verdict; in gene the player WAGERED first, so the stillness either confirms or breaks their bet. Keep gene's stillness always preceded by a placed bet card and ddi's stillness never preceded by a prediction. Different emotional beat (suspense-of-discovery vs payoff-of-a-wager) from the same physical non-move.
- organ vs johanniskraut — both end on a LIVE real-time defence against water heading for a red line (organ up, jk down). SEPARATE on DIRECTION and CAUSE-VISIBILITY: organ rises because an INVISIBLE drain weakened (you turn the tap down), jk falls because a PAST tea is still leaking (you stop the back-dated cause). Keep organ's finale a TIMING catch (tap reduce at the right second) and jk's finale a CAUSE-identification under falling water (lock the right cause, not a moment). They must never both reduce to 'tap the brake in time.'
- adherence vs johanniskraut — both are multi-day 'week' stories that drive the pump day by day. The brief already calls this out; keep it honest: adherence is COMPOSE-THEN-CRANK (zero pressure, you author the curve, additive lesson — more drug always pushes UP), johanniskraut is REACT-UNDER-PRESSURE to a lag you must back-date. Enforce the split in UI: adherence shows an editable 7-slot plan BEFORE playback; johanniskraut never lets you pre-author — its week is fixed history you investigate. Do not let johanniskraut grow a 'build your week' panel.

## Shared grammar (reuse across all six)

- EYES-TO-BODY PROMPT (one convention, one component): a single fixed-position banner 'Schau auf den Körper' / young 'Schau auf den Torso!' that appears at the exact instant any driveTo starts and clears onSettle. Build it once as <WatchBody/> and mount it in all six; never let a story narrate the move with a floating emoji. This directly replaces the current anti-patterns: DdiPlay's '🩸 float', the '…' dots in Gene/Organ/Ddi, and WochePlay's '🎬 + Watch' text. RULE: during any 'moving' beat the screen shows ONLY the watch-body banner + a still caption naming what to look for (e.g. 'Steigt es übers rote Band?') — no emoji animation, because grammar rule 2/9 says the drama is water, not screen.
- NO ON-SCREEN VESSEL — a hard lint rule for the set. Delete GenePlay's mini level-bars (.bar/.fill height) and JkPlay's 'min {level}' numeric meter; both mirror the tank on screen and break grammar rule 2. The only permitted on-screen level reference is qualitative ('hoch/im Grün/nahe der roten Marke'), never a bar, gauge fill, or number. OrganPlay's eGFR gauge is allowed ONLY because it shows kidney function, not the drug LEVEL — keep it visually distinct from a level bar (horizontal needle, labelled eGFR) so visitors never read it as a mirrored torso.
- WIN/LOSS TANK CHOREOGRAPHY (shared): WIN = the last meaningful driveTo SETTLES inside [55,70] and onSettle routes to EndScreen with outcome 'win'; the settle itself is the celebration (slow approach, then rest in green) — no confetti needed. LOSS = either the engine AUTO-TRIPS across 80/35 during a play phase (dramatic, water crosses the red tape) OR a settle lands out-of-band (over/under). Demonstration beats that must NOT lose must cap below 80 / above 35 (organ 78→79.3, jk floor 38, ddi 62→76). Every story already ends on EndScreen — keep that as the single shared outcome surface.
- SCORING SHAPE — one signature, used identically: stars(win, clever, pro) = 1 + clever[0/0.5/1] + pro[0/0.5/1], any loss = 0 (flow.ts). clever = the story's core read done cleanly (full) vs with one stumble/retry (half); pro = the secondary mastery check. FIX: JkPlay currently bypasses this by summing FOUR 0.5 bonuses (1 + [medBonus,trendFirstCorrect,baitCount===0,proBonus]*0.5) — that can exceed the intended shape and is the only story not using starsFor(). Collapse jk to the same two-bonus stars() call so all six score identically and half-stars always mean 'one small stumble.'
- YOUNG/ADULT REGISTER SWITCH — one filter pattern already in use: options/notches/quiz are filtered by `game.ageGroup === 'adult' || !o.adultOnly`, and 'young' gets a visible hint (Organ's pulsing 'reduced' notch). Standardise: (a) every choice list uses the adultOnly filter, (b) young mode always offers exactly one gentle hint affordance per decision, (c) copy keys carry the register, not the logic — same beat structure, two strings. Keep terms concrete for young ('Aufräum-Team', 'Turbo-Maschine') and precise for adult ('CYP3A4/P-gp', 'Ultrarapid-Metabolisierer').
- EYES-TO-BODY HYPOTHESIS PATTERN (the grammar's core loop, made reusable): tap a hypothesis → call driveTo → the WRONG hypothesis passes a target equal to current level (engine moves nothing = instant 'harmlos'), the RIGHT one passes a real target (slow travel). Wire this once as a helper testHypothesis(target, isReal) so fruehstuecks (remove), ddi (scan), gene (bet), adherence ('stop the pill' = no-move) all express 'stillness = not it' through the SAME code path, not bespoke per story.

## Copy-tone consistency

"Three issues across the set. (1) MIXED LANGUAGE IN BRIEFS: ddi and parts of others are written in English while the product is DE-only ('SCAN-A-PAIRING', 'the tank IS the interaction alarm', loss-copy notes). All player-facing copy and the design vocabulary that becomes UI strings must be German; keep English only in code comments. (2) TWO REGISTERS MUST STAY PARALLEL, NOT DRIFT: the set mixes warm-young metaphors ('Aufräum-Team', 'Turbo-Maschine', 'das Leck stopfen') with adult terms ('CYP3A4', 'P-gp', 'Ultrarapid', 'eGFR'). Good — but every beat needs BOTH registers authored, not one. Today the metaphors are richest in johanniskraut/gene and thinnest in ddi/organ; level them so each story has an equally vivid young line and an equally precise adult line. (3) DANGER-LINE NAMING IS INCONSISTENT: 'rote Marke', 'rotes Klebeband', 'rote Linie', 'rote Kante', 'Gefahrenlinie', 'red bleeding line' all appear for the SAME physical red tape. Pick ONE canonical pair — young 'die rote Marke', adult 'die kritische Linie' — and use it everywhere; the tape is the most-referenced object in the exhibit and must have one name. Also standardise the green band as 'das grüne Band' (young) / 'der therapeutische Bereich' (adult). (4) RANK TITLES are already centralised in flow.ts rankKey() — make sure no story hardcodes a rank string; all six must read titles from there so the young/adult rank pairs stay consistent."

- KILL THE FLOATING-EMOJI MOVE BEATS. Replace DdiPlay's '🩸 float' move, the '…' pulse-dots in Ddi/Gene/Organ, and WochePlay's '🎬 + adh.play.watch' with the single shared <WatchBody/> banner ('Schau auf den Körper' / 'Schau auf den Torso!'). During any driveTo the screen must point at the hardware, per grammar rules 2 and 9 — never animate a proxy on screen.
- REMOVE ON-SCREEN MIRRORED VESSELS. Delete GenePlay's mini level-bars (the .bar/.fill height columns) and JkPlay's numeric 'min {level}' meter. These literally mirror the tank and violate the single most-load-bearing rule (rule 1/2). Allowed replacement: qualitative words only ('im Grün', 'nahe der roten Marke').
- UNIFY SCORING ON stars(win, clever, pro). JkPlay is the only story not calling starsFor() with the two-bonus shape — it sums four 0.5 flags (medBonus/trendFirstCorrect/baitCount/proBonus). Refactor to exactly two bonuses so half-stars mean the same thing in all six and the rank titles map correctly.
- CONVERT THE THREE REMAINING SCREEN-QUIZZES INTO TANK-READS. Per the briefs, the redesign deletes: DDI's PairLink + bin-sort finale, Gene's predict-into-bins + strategy multiple-choice, Organ's detective multiple-choice + measure bin-sort, Adherence's closing ADH_QUIZ, and JK's trend multiple-choice + drag-to-slot finale. Every 'richtig/falsch' box must become a pump move (tap hypothesis → stillness or travel). Right now ALL FIVE built screens still ship at least one on-screen quiz; this is the core of the redesign and the biggest delta from code to brief.
- ADD THE MANDATORY ONE-TANK-SURPRISE TWIST PER STORY as a forced demo beat, not a hidden option (ddi's Magenschutz demo is the model). Audit that each story's twist is unavoidable: gene's turbo child must surge live over the red tape (not a preloaded high tank), organ's same-dose-now-rises must be the same untouched tap, adherence's panic-double must climb the wrong way from a topped-up level, fruehstuecks' 'increase dose' must auto-trip over 80, johanniskraut's blame-the-wrong-day must be wrong because the fall is back-dated.
- CANONICALISE THE RED-LINE AND GREEN-BAND NAMES across all copy keys (young 'die rote Marke' / 'das grüne Band'; adult 'die kritische Linie' / 'der therapeutische Bereich'). Replace the current scatter of 'rotes Klebeband / rote Linie / rote Kante / Gefahrenlinie'. One name for the exhibit's most-referenced physical object.
- TRANSLATE ALL DESIGN COPY TO GERMAN before it becomes UI strings — several briefs (especially ddi) are written in English. Product is DE-only; English stays in code comments only.
- EXTRACT A SHARED testHypothesis(target, isReal) HELPER so 'wrong guess = pump doesn't move (instant harmlos)' is one code path used by fruehstuecks, ddi, gene, and adherence's 'stop the pill' no-move, rather than four bespoke implementations. Consistency of the stillness beat is what makes the grammar legible to a visitor across stories.
- LEVEL THE YOUNG/ADULT COPY DEPTH. Every beat needs both a vivid young metaphor and a precise adult term; today johanniskraut/gene are rich and ddi/organ are thin. Pair this with the existing adultOnly filter so structure stays identical and only strings switch.

## Verdict

"As a SET OF DESIGNS (the briefs), yes — this is now exciting and genuinely torso-first. The six signature verbs are distinct on the axes that matter (subtract / back-date-a-lag / bet-then-pour / scan-a-pairing / catch-a-rate / author-time), each has a mandatory one-tank surprise that only the real water can deliver, and the worst overlaps (fruehstuecks↔ddi, adherence↔johanniskraut, organ↔johanniskraut) are separable with the polarity/agency/direction splits and copy locks above. The grammar's spine — tap a hypothesis, let stillness or travel be the verdict — is strong and reusable across all six. BUT this verdict is about the TARGET, not the current build: every one of the five implemented screens still ships the exact flat anti-patterns the redesign exists to kill — on-screen mini level-bars (Gene), a numeric level meter (JK), floating-emoji move beats (Ddi/Woche), and at least one screen-quiz per story (PairLink, bin-sort, multiple-choice). The designs are exciting; the code is still the old decorative-pump version. The set becomes genuinely torso-first only once the cross-story fixes land — chiefly: route every move through a single <WatchBody/> banner, delete all mirrored on-screen vessels, convert all five remaining screen-quizzes into pump-moves, and unify scoring on stars(win,clever,pro). Build ddi first as the reference that proves the shared <WatchBody/> + testHypothesis() + EndScreen pattern, then propagate."
